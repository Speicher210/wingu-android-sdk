ChannelTimestamps

data class ChannelTimestamps

Specifies timestamps associated with a channel.

Parameters

channelId - Channel ID.

discoveredAt - The date when the channel has been discovered, and device is in range of it.

lostAt - The date when the device went out of range of the channel.

notificationCancelledAt - The date when a notification with the channel was cancelled (usually because the channel went out of range).

notificationDismissedAt - The date when a notification with the channel was swiped away by the user.

notifiedAt - The date when a notification for this channel was displayed.

seenAt - The date when the channel's content has been viewed by the user.
