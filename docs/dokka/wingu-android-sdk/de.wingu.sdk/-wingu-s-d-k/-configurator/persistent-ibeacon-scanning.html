persistentIbeaconScanning

abstract var persistentIbeaconScanning: Boolean

If true, and WinguSDKBuilder.ibeaconBackgroundScan is enabled, then the app starts a foreground service (with a notification) and is not influenced by Doze or App Standby, but may consume more battery.
